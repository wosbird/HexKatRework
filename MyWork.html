I do user experience design from beginning to end, problem to solution, and accessibility is my #1 priority. I analyze problems and find solutions, either with a team or flying solo. I am always striving to improve all my sills, but there are a few that I particularly enjoy. For example...

Because of the analytical nature, I find building and rebuilding the information archtecture of sites is more than necessary but also a fun activity. I enjoy the categorization process, the analysis and creativity involved, and the focus on the user's mindset and needs.

Thorough usability testing is key to creating an accesible, user-centered product. I enjoy the process of presenting a product in-progress to potential users, receiving feed, and iterating the project. The insight of people outside of the design studeio is invaluable.
